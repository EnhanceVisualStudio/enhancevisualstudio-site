What to expect after booking

A smooth client experience matters just as much as the images themselves.

1. Confirmation You’ll receive an email confirming your selected date, time, and session type.
2. Session planning We’ll finalize location, wardrobe direction, mood, and any key visual ideas for the shoot.
3. Shoot day Your session is guided with a balance of direction, natural energy, and polished creative execution.
4. Delivery Edited images are delivered in a polished online gallery within your stated turnaround window.

Booking policy

Clear expectations help protect both the client experience and the creative process.

Retainer

A non-refundable retainer is required to secure your booking date and time.

Remaining balance

The remaining balance is due on or before the day of the shoot unless otherwise agreed in writing.

Rescheduling

Sessions may be rescheduled with at least 48 hours notice, subject to availability.

Turnaround time

Edited images are typically delivered within 5–7 days unless your package states otherwise.

Custom events

Larger events, weddings, or specialized productions may require a custom quote before booking is confirmed.
